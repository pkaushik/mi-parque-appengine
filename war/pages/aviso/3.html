Fire Evacuation Plan

In September, a fire broke out at the Crawford Coal Power Plant. As a result, residents realized that there is no evacuation plan for Little Village. A public meeting will take place the Little Village Social Justice High School Auditorium at 7:00 on October 21st to discuss with our alderman and city officials.
